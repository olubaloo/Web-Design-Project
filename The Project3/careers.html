Careers

A career with us is about finding a home for the passion you bring to your work, a place where other people share your aspirations. At Chellarams, we are known for our high level of commitment, we give everyone the opportunity to learn and progress. We provide continuous on the job training, as well as workshops and seminars led by external experts.

You will have a clear mapped out path of development, where you will have the support of your manager to enable you to be successful. We offer extensive training to all our staff, wherever you are starting, you are assured of the best possible start.

Our culture celebrates diversity and diverse perspectives; it helps employees achieve an effective mix between work and home life, and supports their efforts to have a positive impact on their communities.

Working with us, you can look forward to a wealth of learning opportunity which will equip you with all the skills and confidence you will need to be able to fulfil your potential. We invite you to take a closer look at our family of companies and to be sure our goals and values align with your aspirations.



Current Job Opportunities
Chellarams Plc conducts business in Nigeria and offers you diverse opportunities for continued growth – both professionally and personally. You’ll get the opportunity to work with colleagues from all over the world and work in all the locations and communities in which we operate. We offer highly attractive rewards packages and we undertake regular reviews to ensure our salaries remain sufficiently competitive.
